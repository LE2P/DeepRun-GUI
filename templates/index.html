Deep Learning for Reunion Energy Autonomy

DeepRun (Deep Learning for Reunion Energy Autonomy) is a postdoctoral project conducted by the University of La Réunion and supervised by the ENERGY-lab laboratory and CIRAD.

Main objective
Development of transversal deep machine learning algorithms for image recognition for various types of applications, from bubbly regimes for the tropicalisation of decarbonised hydrogen production, to the use of intertropical agricultural land for the optimisation of plant biomass.

Features
This API provides some useful tools related to AI world for data analysis. It's composed of four main parts: the first part "Modeling" is the model instancing, the second part "Training" is a training process, the third part "inference" is the model exploitation, and the last part "Tools" includes a variety of miscellaneous method related to DeepRun functions.
